003 What is a fuse mechanism?什么是熔断机制?
2020-07-25

据纽约证券交易所称，标普500的“三个熔断点”可能会因为大幅下跌和波动而暂停交易。根据标普500前一交易日的收盘价，纽交所将其分为三个等级。

以下是只适用于正常交易时间的规则:

一级熔断:如果标普500下跌7%，交易将暂停15分钟。

二级熔断:如果标普500下跌13%，且下跌发生在美国东部时间下午3点25分或之前，交易将再次暂停15分钟。如果在那之后下跌，交易将不会停止。

三级熔断:如果标普500下跌20%，当天交易将暂停。(如果标普500指数下跌594点，就会出现这种情况)。

这些断路器从未在正常交易时间以当前的形式被触发。先前的断路器系统在未能阻止2010年5月的闪存崩盘后进行了改进。目前的这套熔断机制是在2013年2月开始生效的。

投资者在开盘时就知道，这种停顿是基于隔夜期货交易的一种可能性

Trading on the S & P 500’s “three melting points” could be suspended due to sharp falls and volatility, according to the New York Stock Exchange. The New York Stock Exchange classifies the S & P 500 into three tiers based on its closing price in the previous session.

The following rules apply only to normal trading hours:

Primary Circuit Breaker: If the S & P 500 falls 7 per cent, trading will be suspended for 15 minutes.

Secondary Circuit Breaker: If the S & P 500 is down 13% and the decline occurs at or before 3:25 pm EST, trading will be suspended for another 15 minutes. If it falls after that, trading will not stop.

THREE-TIER FUSES: If the S & P 500 falls 20 per cent, trading will be suspended for the day. That’s what happens when the S & P 500 drops 594 points.

These circuit breakers have never been triggered in their current form during normal trading hours. Previous circuit breaker systems were improved after failing to prevent a flash MEMORY CRASH IN MAY 2010. The current circuit breaker went into effect in February 2013.
